The Story of KITASEMUA

Mera Kwan grew up in a tight-knit community where many realized that traditional insurance models often left vulnerable individuals without the protection they needed. Determined to make a difference, Mera envisioned an insurance company that used technology to make insurance simple, affordable, and accessible for everyone. Mera partnered with Marty, a talented technology expert who built the micro-investment app Acorns in the US, valued at 2 billion USD, to bring the vision to life. Together, they created KITASEMUA, a platform designed to eliminate the need for expensive agents and time-consuming paperwork. The name, meaning "all of us" in Indonesian, reflects their commitment to inclusivity and community support.
Their vision is to create a world where everyone feels secure and supported. KITASEMUA's mission is to provide accessible and reliable insurance services that cater to all individuals, regardless of their background or status. Mera and Marty believe that everyone deserves the protection and security to live their best lives, and KITASEMUA is dedicated to making that a reality.

KITASEMUA is a life insurance agency that collaborates with renowned global insurance companies, enhancing services by utilizing AI technology to simplify access to insurance for all Indonesians. We believe that everyone deserves protection and peace of mind in their lives.
